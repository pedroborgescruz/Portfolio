Born & raised in São Paulo, Brazil, I'm currently a senior at Swarthmore College majoring in computer science. I'm passionate about tech and aim to learn from both industry and academia to drive innovation. I'm especially intrigued by how machine learning can break down language barriers in natural language applications. I'm also excited by the impact of changing how we think about money managament through fintechs and the challenges of space communications.

I'm also very interested in computer science and linguistics research. I have experience working with computer networking and open-source software development in academia. During my summers, I was able to explore different positions in a company's pipeline, ranging from operations to product and development. As a new-grad, I'm now ready to focus towards a more technical career.
